About Us


TFF Trading Cards has founded by Omay Ece Ayyıldız, Berkant Kartop, Oğuzhan Kaya. It is an eCommerce site for buying trading cards of Turkish football players. The main goal of the founders was bringing the trading card culture to Turkey. Trading cards are one of the simple prodcuts which can be considered team merchandise, but today they have became a physical version of NFT's(Non Fungeable Tokens). Trading Cards market had growth 6.1 billion US dollars. This kind of a market can be created in Turkey too.

In 2016 before a match, 5 thousand turkey national team jerseys had sold with the price tag of 140 turkish liras. Turkish football fans are willing to support their team in any way possible due to their love for football. Since there is no competion Turkish Football Federation can dominate the market easily.
